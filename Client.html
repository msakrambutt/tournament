Clients

Consider a Building Management System when you:

  • want to strategically and centrally manage energy across your organization.
  • have existing energy monitoring systems and notice unexpected increases in your energy usage.
  • are managing multiple facilities or systems and experience problems with equipment and system performance.
  • work to ensure compliance with building codes and regulations or are aiming for green building certification.

Hotels

Vantage controls and optimizes room comfort levels to save energy costs while enhancing guest experience. Extensive energy savings are achieved on a room level, on the air-conditioning systems in suites, and at a hotel level by reducing the energy consumption of the water heat pumps, by using our proprietary Vantage system.

Hotels

Shopping Malls

Multiple tenants can be billed on actual air, electricity and water usage, instead of on a generic square foot basis. Savings are achieved by load managing in real time, providing utilities on a predictive managed basis instead of responding to user triggered demand surges.

Shopping Malls

Hospitals

Critical environments are controlled to temperatures in each theatre instead of set points on the controller, providing exact control for sensitive clinical settings.

Hospitals

College Dormitories and Lecture Halls

Vantage monitors and controls campuses and buildings, with consolidated views of multi-site operations. Considerable savings are achieved by controlling and optimising hot water to users, identifying leaks, pinpointing load balancing issues, and prompting predictive maintenance.

College Dormitories and Lecture Halls

Cold Storage

Monitor and control compressors to optimise the energy consumption and compressor life and save money immediately while providing a competitive edge with consistent high-quality cooling for products.

Cold Storage

Sport Stadiums

Stadiums are heavy consumers of energy for their operations, due to the use of floodlights, indoor and outdoor lighting, and heavy equipment. To optimize the use of energy for these activities, stadiums use Vantage to help stadium operators with the efficient management of facilities and associated activities.

Sport Stadiums
