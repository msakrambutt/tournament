Predictive Analysis

Our smart technology combines data mining, statistics, modelling and forecasting intelligence to analyse current and past energy data to predict your buildings future performance. Using this data, we can identify trends and uncover optimisation opportunities. For example, by monitoring weather patterns we can preheat buildings during off peak times to reduce demand-related energy costs.
