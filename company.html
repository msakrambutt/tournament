About

Our Mission

To provide an uncomplicated, simple to implement, cost-effective software platform that provides impressive financial returns for our customers.

We serve the hospitality, education, healthcare and industrial sectors, amongst others.

Energy Solutions evolved in 2010 to develop and supply a Building Management System that was not based around the sale of hardware. We developed the Vantage software platform in-house, to provide customers with an open source platform that clients can connect to any device, sensor or network and have a platform that can be viewed from anywhere in the world, with the ability to track performance and print reports from one portal.
