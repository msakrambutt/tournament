Why use Vantage Energy Solutions:

At Energy Solutions we believe there is a simpler way of delivering Building Management Solutions by reducing the complexity and providing a simple road path to monitoring and controlling a business environment. The three significant differentiators which make ES unique are:

  • Vantage is an open source solution. The platform is interoperable, ensuring clients own their data and can be utilized in conjunction with 3rd party platforms.
  • Selective applications. This allows clients to identify specific needs or issues within their buildings, which we focus on to provide a solution. Other issues can be tackled later.
  • Highly scalable. The application can be ‘added to’ seamlessly, and at any rate.

Vantage

ES’ powerful software platform - Vantage - allows customers to execute energy efficiency strategies at their own pace. Our end-to-end solution addresses challenges in the building automation industry by presenting data on our platform, on the edge or in the cloud environment. Devices can stream critical data on energy usage and site conditions through the Vantage platform, which allows our Data Analytics Engine to track patterns, predict behaviors, and calculate outputs. Our system scales effortlessly, with real-time dashboards that are available from any location. The system has been designed by our team to interoperate with existing instrumentation, control, and IT systems, reducing implementation costs and accelerating project rollouts.

View and Control

Our software platform provides clients with a real-time view of their business from any location. The system facilitates the option of switching equipment manually, from remote locations. Application examples include: equipment runtimes, tank levels, irregular power usage, water leaks, temperature levels, automatic meter reading, HVAC monitoring, alarms and notifications.

Save

Increased monetary savings are achieved with our control hardware and Vantage software that uses Machine Learning Algorithms and Edge Processing software engines to reduce clients’ environmental footprint by managing the use of various infrastructure components being: power, water, natural gas, waste, and mechanical operations.

Optimize

Vantage building optimization system extends automated control to all aspects of your operation on a single platform – generating data that is analysed to:

  • Enhance comfort, safety, and productivity for occupants.
  • Predict failures and prevent downtime by scheduling pre-emptive maintenance.
  • Optimising efficiency with industrial level automation systems for entire facilities and campuses.
