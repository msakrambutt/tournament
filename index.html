Why use Macrofinsights Financial Consultancy:

At Macrofinsights we believe there is a simpler way of delivering Building Management Solutions by reducing the complexity and providing a simple road path to monitoring and controlling a business environment. With this thought in mind, we developed a sophisticated but simple-to-use software platform - Vantage - that provides customers with a real-time of view of their business, while we do the heavy lifting in the background. The three significant differentiators which make ES unique are:

  • Macrofinsights is an open source solution. The platform is interoperable, ensuring clients own their data and can be utilized in conjunction with 3rd party platforms.
  • Selective applications. This allows clients to identify specific needs or issues within their buildings, which we focus on to provide a solution. Other issues can be tackled later.
  • Highly scalable. The application can be ‘added to’ seamlessly, and at any rate.

Macrofinsights

ES’ powerful software platform - Macrofinsights - allows customers to execute energy efficiency strategies at their own pace.
